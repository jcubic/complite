This site is a static HTML website. It does not use cookies, analytics scripts, tracking pixels, or third-party advertising. Your visit is not logged by default.

## Contact form

When you use the [contact form](/contact/), the information you submit (name, email, subject, message) is sent via email to the site owner. This data is used only to reply to you and is not stored in any database, shared with third parties, or used for marketing.

## Subscribe form

The subscribe form in the footer is a placeholder. If the site owner connects it to a newsletter service, that service's privacy policy will apply. By default, no data is collected.

## Hosting

This site may be hosted on a server that collects standard HTTP access logs (IP address, user agent, timestamp, requested URL). These logs are used for server maintenance and security, not for tracking individual visitors.

## External resources

This site loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google's [privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) applies to those requests.

## Your rights

If you've submitted data through the contact form and want it deleted, [get in touch](/contact/) and it will be handled promptly.
